Our Medical Services

1. Treatment of Severe Malaria and Its Associated Complications

Severe malaria remains the number one killer in our community. At Dza Kwelin Children's Hospital, we approach diagnosis and treatment with urgency and precision.

Our rapid diagnostic tests and microscopic blood smears confirm the parasite presence and density. Intravenous artesunate is our first-line treatment, delivered swiftly to stop the infection in its tracks.

Severe malaria often leads to life-threatening anemia, which is why we prioritize timely, safe blood transfusions. Our team ensures rigorous blood typing, cross-matching, and careful monitoring to improve patient outcomes and save lives.

2. Treatment of Various Pediatric Infections

From meningitis to skin infections and abscesses, our hospital uses a comprehensive strategy to treat a wide array of childhood infections.

Lumbar punctures and CSF analysis enable fast meningitis diagnosis. URTIs like pneumonia and TB are managed with targeted antibiotics and supportive care.

We treat UTIs, salmonellosis, and other bacterial infections with evidence-based therapy. Skin infections like impetigo and cellulitis are treated based on severity with topical or systemic medications.

3. Emergency Management of Common Pediatric Ailments

The Dza Kwelin Children's Hospital is equipped to handle a range of common pediatric emergencies with prompt and efficient care.

For severe respiratory distress, immediate interventions include oxygen therapy, nebulization, and, if necessary, intubation and ventilation. Febrile seizures are managed with antipyretics and anticonvulsants, with thorough investigations to identify the underlying cause.

In cases of severe dehydration due to vomiting or diarrhea, rapid fluid resuscitation via IV lines or oral rehydration therapy is prioritized.
