Our Mission

Founded in 2019 by the Salaam Doctors' Foundation, Dza Kwelin Children's Hospital is dedicated to delivering equitable, affordable, and accessible healthcare to every child in Cameroon. We aim to bridge the healthcare gap by offering high-quality pediatric services to the most vulnerable, especially the internally displaced and underserved communities.

Our Impact

From a humble 10-bed beginning, our hospital has grown to a 28-bed facility, staffed by volunteer pediatricians, general surgeons, and physicians. Our mission is supported by a compassionate nursing team that ensures holistic care for every child.
